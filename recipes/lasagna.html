Odin's Favorite Lasagna

Description

This creamy lasagna is fit for a king - or the king of the gods! You'll impress anyone with this recipe that combines all the comforting flavors into one punchy, creamy dish.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella (plus more for the top)
  • 3/4 cups parmesan
  • 2 large jars of meat sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. Mix the ricotta, egg, 2 cups mozzarella, 3/4 cups parmesan in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Add a layer of sauce to the bottom of a greased pan, then start your first layer of noodles. Make sure to prep the noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Spread the cheese mixture over the noodles you layed down, then follow with another layer of sauce. Follow this pattern until you use up the noodles.
  4. Top your last noodles with sauce, then top that with mozzarella. Cover with foil and add to a 425F oven for like an hour.
